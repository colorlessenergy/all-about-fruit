Cabbage is a leafy green or purple plant, grown as an annual vegetable crop for its dense-leaved heads.

The Orange

The orange fruit is one of the most eaten fruits in the Unites States.

It is a member of the citrus family along with lemons, limes and grapefruit.

Oranges are most often inside a thick, orangey-yellow skin, and the inside is separated into segments of juicy pulp.
